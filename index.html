About the business

Asphalt Kings of central Pennsylvania is an asphalt paving company out of Altoona, Pennsylvania and is a family-owned and operated business. It is managed & operated by Nathan LaPenna and Shanta Grubb right out of their home in Altoona, PA. The business was established in July of 2020 and most of the day to day is ran by Nathan, who has over 12 years of asphalt & paving experience.

Services they offer

This family-based business offers several asphalt paving and seal coating services. They also offer maintenence and repair services as well. Asphalt Kings does both commercial and private asphalt, paving, and seal coating projects. No job is too big or small for this company.
